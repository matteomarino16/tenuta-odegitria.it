— THE VENUE

An authentic place for your special day

Tenuta Odegitria offers wide outdoor areas and alleys between the trulli, the garden around the pool, as well as a large wooden gazebo, for up to about 80 guests for events, exhibitions, conferences and weddings.

It is officially recognized as a separate Civil Registry Office for the celebration of weddings and civil unions pursuant to art. 3 of DPR 396/2000.

Nearby you will find the Church of Sant’Antonio ai Cappuccini, devoted to the Madonna dell’Odegitria, as well as the historic Baroque churches of Martina Franca for religious ceremonies.

Lunch or dinner is provided by an external banqueting & catering service.
