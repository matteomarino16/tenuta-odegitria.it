— TENUTA ODEGITRIA

Tenuta Odegitria was born from the owners’ wish to bring back to life the traditions of the past and a cultural heritage that reflects the simplicity and hard work of the local community.

The owners’ family (two doctor parents and their two children) lives in the central complex of trulli, “I Trulli del Massaro”, and is committed to offering guests a pleasant and peaceful stay, while respecting privacy, with B&B or holiday-home services among the scents and flavors of Puglia.

— EVENTS & WEDDINGS

Tenuta Odegitria offers wide outdoor areas and alleys between the trulli, the garden around the pool, as well as a large wooden gazebo, for up to about 80 guests for events, exhibitions, conferences and weddings.

It is officially recognized as a separate Civil Registry Office for the celebration of weddings and civil unions pursuant to art. 3 of DPR 396/2000.

Nearby you will find the Church of Sant’Antonio ai Cappuccini, devoted to the Madonna dell’Odegitria, as well as the historic Baroque churches of Martina Franca for religious ceremonies. Lunch or dinner is provided by an external banqueting & catering service.
